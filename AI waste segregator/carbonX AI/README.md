# 🌿 carbonX AI 
### *Empowering Sustainability through Artificial Intelligence*

carbonX AI is a comprehensive dashboard designed to gamify and simplify environmental conservation. By combining Computer Vision with data-driven footprint analysis, we help users transition from "awareness" to "action."

---

##  Key Features

### 1.  AI Waste Classifier
Uses a **MobileNetV2** Deep Learning model (PyTorch) to identify waste materials in real-time. 
- **Instant Recognition:** Upload a photo of trash, and the AI identifies the object.
- **Smart Mapping:** Automatically categorizes items into Plastic, Paper, E-waste, Organic, or Glass.

### 2.  Carbon Footprint Tracker
An interactive calculator that visualizes your environmental impact across four pillars:
- **Transport:** Weekly mileage tracking.
- **Energy:** Monthly kWh consumption.
- **Diet:** Impact of meat-based vs. plant-based meals.
- **Consumption:** Waste generated from new purchases.

### 3.  Eco-Warrior Leaderboard
A gamified social system where users earn "Green Points" (XP) for every item recycled and every footprint milestone reached.

---

##  Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** [Streamlit](https://streamlit.io/) (Python-based Web Framework)
- **AI/ML:** [PyTorch](https://pytorch.org/) & `torchvision`
- **Data Viz:** [Plotly](https://plotly.com/python/)
- **Image Processing:** PIL (Pillow)
